Here Some Steps to Travel...

STEP 1

Visit the official website and navigate the travel insurance dropdown. Select the country you wish to visit.

STEP 2

Once on the desired page, navigate to the right side of the screen, enter your phone number , and click on the "calculate premium" button.

STEP 3

Follow the form flow and provide details such as the proposer's age and insured member(s) details in the designated columns.

STEP 4

Select if the insured members suffer from pre-existing diseases. Select the desired amount of sum insured and proceed further.

FINISH

Once you click on the "get a quote" button, the premium amount will appear on the screen with the critical USPs of the plan. Proceed to make the payment and save a receipt for further reference.
